Engaged Mode

GUESTS HAVE COMPLETE CONTROL

In this mode you can thumbs up and down the next track to determine if it plays next. The user will have the entire duration of the currently playing song to vote. If the next track has more thumbs down votes when the current song stops then it will move to the end of queue!

Classic Mode

THIS IS YOUR CLASSIC JUKEBOX TODAY

In classic mode you will have access to the hosts complete music library and you can add any songs you like to the party playlist. Tracks will be played in the order they are added to the queue. Step up that music game!

Host Settings

MINIMAL SETTINGS FOR MAXIMUM FUN

We want you to focus on making sure your guests have the best time so we kept the settings to the absolute minimum. You can control how many songs your guests can add per hour, the proximity range, the ability to play explicit content and of course a crossfade option.
